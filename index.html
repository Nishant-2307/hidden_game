The Hidden
Card Game

Ten cards. Three players. One hidden. Use logical queries to deduce the mystery card before your opponents do.

How to Play

  • Cards 1–10 are dealt: 3 to you, 3 to Bot 1, 3 to Bot 2, and 1 remains hidden.
  • Each turn, pick one of the 5 query sets (each containing 4 cards) and ask a bot how many of those 4 cards they hold.
  • Use the answers to eliminate possibilities and deduce the hidden card.
  • The first player who can identify the hidden card with certainty wins!
  • Both bots play optimally using information theory — choose your queries wisely.
